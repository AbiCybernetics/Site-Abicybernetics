AbiCybernetics Exoskeleton – Mobility for you

A modular platform for assisted walking high quality materials, ready for different sensor integrations. Real time synchronized control for natural gait.

Walking is Human

Walking is one of the simplest yet most profound human acts. We rarely think about it — it’s part of who we are. With every step we feel the ground, move through the world, meet people and build experiences. Walking means freedom, independence and dignity.

For millions of people living with paraplegia or severe mobility impairments, walking is a distant dream. Being confined to a wheelchair is not only a loss of mobility — it carries a heavy toll on overall health. Prolonged sitting affects circulation, the heart, muscle tone and even mental wellbeing. For healthy people we say “sitting is the new smoking.” For someone with a motor disability, sitting becomes a true killer, accelerating complications and reducing both lifespan and quality of life. That’s why exoskeleton technology isn’t just robotics — it’s a promise of life.

How the exoskeleton assists gait

4 motors

Four high‑precision actuators at the hips and knees deliver the torque needed for standing up and smooth gait.

Support crutches

Added balance and fine control enable safe, assisted walking for users with gait impairments.

Sensing

Sensors track posture and user intent in real time and adapt each step accordingly.

Real‑time control

Millisecond‑level synchronization avoids rigidity and aims for a natural walking pattern.

* Note: device under development; not certified as a medical device.

Modular sensor platform

  • EEG (electroencephalography) – interprets brain signals to anticipate user intent.
  • EMG (electromyography) – provides muscular feedback and detects spasms from the user.
  • Laser sensors – perceive the environment and adapt automatically to terrain.
  • Tilt sensors – adjust gait dynamically according to ground angle and slope.

Premium materials & ergonomics

  • High grade aluminum frame — a medical/aerospace-grade alloy with excellent strength-to-weight ratio.
  • Robust yet lightweight — durable but light enough for everyday wear.
  • Ergonomic padding — placed at contact points to maximize comfort during longer sessions.
  • High build quality — ensures daily usability, safety, and reliability.
