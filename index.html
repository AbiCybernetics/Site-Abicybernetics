Intelligence at core

Designed for versatility, the system supports a wide range of sensor integrations — EEG, EMG, force, gyroscope, laser, and more. Whether you are monitoring neural activity, movement, or force feedback, the platform is ready to meet these needs. Its architecture allows you to tailor detection capabilities to individual requirements, research objectives, or evolving use cases. It’s not just intelligent — it’s built for the precision you need.

Rapid setup

Every component of the exoskeleton is designed to snap easily into place — no tools, no tangled cables. The modular design makes setup fast and intuitive, whether you are preparing for a mobility session, clinical use, or a research setup. You have a full-body system that is easy to wear and simple to adjust.

Extended Battery Life

Enjoy extended battery autonomy for all-day use. Walk for up to 2 hours continuously or up to 5 hours with breaks, and when it’s time to recharge, simply swap the battery in seconds. The quick-change battery means zero interruptions — just swap and keep going.

Ergonomic Excellence

The exoskeleton allows fine adjustments of joint positions, segment lengths, and alignment angles to adapt to individual anatomical differences. It is designed to provide safe and comfortable support for users with heights between 150 cm and 190 cm and body weights of up to 100 kg, ensuring optimal fit, functionality, and biomechanical efficiency for a wide range of users.
