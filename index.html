Compact modular system

Built for freedom, research, and everyday exploration.

Abi combines modular engineering with human-centered design to create a lower-body exoskeleton that is compact, portable, and adaptable for a wide range of users and use cases.

  • Human-centered fit – fine adjustments of joint positions, segment lengths, and alignment angles help adapt the system to individual anatomical differences.
  • Portable by design – a compact full-body system that is modular, portable, easy to carry, and fast to wear.
  • Designed for pioneers – created for early adopters, researchers, and innovators who want to explore movement and test new technologies.
Intelligence at core
