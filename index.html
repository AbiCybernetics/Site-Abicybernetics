Core highlights

Four capabilities that make the platform feel future-ready.

The new landing experience focuses on clarity, depth, and confidence: high-contrast visuals, elevated content hierarchy, and concise proof points that frame Abi as a premium technology product.

Intelligent sensing backbone

Support for EEG, EMG, force, gyroscope, laser, and other integrations gives teams a platform that is ready for research, analytics, and evolving mobility workflows.

Fast modular setup

Each component is designed to lock into place quickly, reducing friction between transport, fitting, and the moment the user starts moving.

All-day flexibility

Continuous use up to 2 hours or up to 5 hours with breaks, plus quick battery swaps, keeps the experience practical instead of purely aspirational.

Ergonomic precision

Fine adjustment across joints, segment lengths, and alignment angles supports a secure, biomechanically informed fit for a wide range of users.
